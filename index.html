Stephen Lee

BIO

I am a Ph.D. Student at University of Massachuestts Amherst. My advisor is Prof. Prashant Shenoy.

My research interests lie broadly in the area of distributed systems and cyber-physical systems (CPS), with an emphasis on smart cities, energy and transportation. My work involves using principled approaches from distributed systems, machine learning, and optimization to optimize for energy efficiency, dependability of systems, and costs.

I also maintain the Smart* repository.
